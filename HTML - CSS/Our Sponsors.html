| Our Sponsors |

Porsche's renowned status has secured us incredible sponsors. The major

| Michelin |

Porsche and Michelin have a longstanding and excellent partnership. As a
sponsor, Michelin supports Porsche via supplying top of the line tyres to
help Porsche reach the esteemed requirments that are desired. This allows for
Porsche vehicles to have superb handling due to the manufacturing of tyres specific
for every model.

| Veltins |

Porsche's sponsor, VELTINS, a renowned German brewery, enhances the brand's

This partnership benefits each other in terms of visibility and customer engagement.
